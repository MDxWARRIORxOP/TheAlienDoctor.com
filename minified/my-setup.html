PC Parts

Below is all the parts for my custom build pc! Originally made by me at the end of 2020, I have recorded on the same computer since then. Throughout that time it has had many upgrades and changes. I have worked hard to get this pc (and entire setup) and absolutely love it!

You can also find this computer on PC PCPartPickerhere!

CPU: Ryzen 7 3800x

  • Cores: 8
  • Threads: 16
  • Core Clock: 3.9 GHz
  • Boost Clock: 4.5 GHz

GPU: EVGA RTX 2080 SUPER BLACK

  • Memory: 8 GB
  • Core Clock: 1650 MHz
  • Boost Clock: 1815 MHz

Motherboard: MSI MAG B550 Tomahawk Max

  • Form Factor: ATX
  • Chipset: AMD B550
  • Memory Slots: 4

RAM: Corsair Vengeance LPX 16 GB (2 x 8 GB)

  • Amount: 2 x 8 GB
  • Speed: 3600

Storage: 2 x 1 TB SSD, 480 GB SSD, 1 TB HDD

  • Boot Drive:WD Blue 1 TB M.2 (Sata) SSD
  • Content/Editing:Sabrent Rocket 1 TB M.2 (NVME) SSD
  • Archive: 1 TB 2.5inch Hard Drive that I pulled out of an old laptop!
  • General storage:Kingston A400 480 GB Sata SSD
